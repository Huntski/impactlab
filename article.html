Nitrogen

One of the biggest environmental problems that we face today has to do with Nitrogen. This is a chemical connection that is destroying the nature around us. This is why we are trying to put a stop to this.

Why its an issue

Nitrogen is a gas that gets emitted by cars, industry and cattle. Too much nitrogen in the air and in the ground will mean that many plants will not grow as well as they used to. This is a problem for many reasons. One of those reasons is that The plants that do thrive in a Nitrogen filled ground are very unwanted plants. Some examples of these plants are brambles and nettles. When these plants do well others will start to die out. This will decrease biodiversity in such a way that we will lose the original nature that once was. The second problem with this is that many animals (mostly insects) Will not be able to live there like they originally did. This will further damage the surrounding aria. Basically if we don’t lower the nitrogen emissions, nature like we once knew will die out.
